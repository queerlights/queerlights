The artist behind Queer Lights

KamionFox

Bold. Provocative.
Unapologetically Queer.

Kamion Fox is a Paris-based digital collagiste working at the intersection of pop art, queer identity, and raw desire. Each piece is built by layering AI-generated characters with found digital fragments — vintage textures, neon glitches, unexpected icons — colliding until something new and electric emerges.

The work doesn't ask for permission. It celebrates masculine beauty, queer sensuality, and the freedom to exist loudly — in large-format canvas prints that command a room, and in stickers that carry that energy into everyday life.

Based in Paris, Kamion creates from a place of deep personal conviction: that art should be felt in the body before it's understood by the mind.

"My art is a love letter to the LGBTQ+ community — raw, electric, and unfiltered. Each piece invites you into a world where pleasure meets artistry, where the body becomes a canvas for liberation."

— Kamion Fox
