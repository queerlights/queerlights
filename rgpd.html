PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 2024

1. Who We Are

Queer Lights is an independent art project by Kamion Fox, based in Paris, France. This website (queerlights.com) is operated as part of an auto-entrepreneur activity registered in France.

Contact: contact@queerlights.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate this website:

  • Contact form: Your name, email address, and message when you contact us voluntarily.
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for site functionality and optional analytics cookies (only with your consent).
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by PayPal. We do not store any payment or card details.
3. How We Use Your Data
  • To respond to your messages and commission requests
  • To process payments via PayPal
  • To improve the website through anonymous analytics
  • We never sell your data to third parties
4. Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

Essential cookies — Required for the site to work (age verification, language preference, cookie consent). Cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies — Optional. Used to understand how visitors use the site. Only activated with your explicit consent. No personally identifiable data is collected.

Cookie preferences are stored for 12 months. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

5. Data Retention

Contact form messages are kept for up to 12 months and then deleted. Cookie preferences are stored locally in your browser for 12 months. We do not maintain a database of personal information.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the CNIL (Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés)
7. Third-Party Services
